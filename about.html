Who I Am

I came to the Bible
looking for God.

Not inspiration. Not comfort. Not a framework for better living. I came looking for God, and what I found changed everything.

Every page of Scripture tells one story.

It is the story of a holy God who, before the foundation of the world, purposed to glorify Himself by redeeming a people through His Son, Jesus Christ. That conviction shapes the way we read the Bible, the way we serve others, and the way we live our lives together.

We are George and Prossy Ssebyala, united by faith, ministry, and a shared desire to see people know Christ more deeply. We believe the Bible is not a collection of disconnected stories but one unfolding account of God's glory revealed in Jesus Christ. For that reason, we approach Scripture carefully, studying it book by book and chapter by chapter, seeking to understand God's purposes and apply His truth faithfully.

Our desire is not merely to gain knowledge but to be transformed by truth. We believe the study of God's Word should lead to worship, humility, obedience, and a deeper love for Christ and His church.

This conviction gave birth to Go & Train, an initiative dedicated to equipping rural pastors and church leaders who have limited access to theological training. Through teaching, discipleship, and practical ministry resources, we seek to strengthen local churches by helping leaders handle God's Word with clarity, faithfulness, and confidence.

George serves through writing, theological study, technology, and education. As a web developer and data analyst, he enjoys building useful tools and solving practical problems through technology. He writes on Scripture, ministry, Christian living, and the ways faith shapes everyday life. He also carries a long-term vision called HopeAbound, a dream of serving communities facing poverty through discipleship, education, practical support, and Christ-centered hope.

Prossy is a devoted follower of Christ whose heart is especially drawn to women and children. She is passionate about encouraging women to grow in faith, confidence, wisdom, and biblical understanding while helping children discover the love and truth of God from an early age. Her ministry is marked by compassion, encouragement, and a desire to see people flourish in Christ.

Together, we seek to serve wherever God provides opportunity. Whether through biblical teaching, ministry training, writing, technology, women's discipleship, children's ministry, or community outreach, our prayer is that everything we do would point people to Jesus Christ and bring glory to God.

This website is an extension of that calling. It is a place where we share what we are learning, what we are building, and what God is teaching us through His Word and His work in our lives.

We are grateful that you are here and invite you to join us on the journey as we pursue Christ and seek to serve His people.
